### Stakeholders
- SOC Team / Analysts
- Security Lead / Manager
- IT / DevOps Team
- Legal & Compliance
- Management / Executive Team

### Communication Guidelines
- Use **secure channels** (encrypted messaging, internal dashboards)
- Provide **clear and concise updates**: severity, affected assets, actions taken
- Share **actionable instructions**, not raw technical data
- Avoid speculation; report only verified information

### Update Frequency
- **Critical incidents:** every 15â€“30 minutes during active exploitation
- **High severity:** hourly updates
- **Medium/Low severity:** end-of-day summary or as needed

## ðŸ§© External Communication

### When to Communicate
- User funds are affected or at risk
- Regulatory notification is required
- Public security advisory is warranted
- Coordinating with exchanges, auditors, or partners

### Guidelines
- Maintain **professional and transparent messaging**
- Avoid disclosing technical details that could assist attackers
- Coordinate messaging with Legal & PR teams
- Provide **next steps and expected timelines** for users

### Suggested Channels
- Official announcements (website, blog, social media)
- Email notifications to affected users
- Coordination with partner exchanges or blockchain analytics firms
